Hi, I'm Maureen Waithaka

Why Digital Pathology?

I am a passionate digital pathologist, dedicated to revolutionizing diagnostics through innovation and technology. My mission is to leverage digital pathology to enhance accuracy, accessibility, and efficiency of surgical pathology diagnosis, ensuring better patient outcomes. I am also deeply committed to medical education, through which we can empower the next generation of pathologists and healthcare professionals.

How I Got Here

My journey in medicine began as a medical intern at a hospital in Meru County, Kenya. The work was profoundly meaningful, particularly seeing patients from distant areas receive good quality care at our facility. Unfortunately, this experience also revealed challenges in our healthcare system. Many patients, especially those in underserved communities, have limited access to surgical pathology diagnoses. This type of diagnosis is critical for effective cancer management. We simply don't have enough pathologists to serve our growing populations. Digital pathology offers a powerful solution to bridge these crucial gaps in care.
